ARKNIGHTS

A mobile tower defense (TD) game developed by Hypergryph, originally released in mainland China on May 1, 2019,

before receiving a release in Japan, Korea, North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia (collectively known as Global servers) by Yostar on January 16, 2020.

On the 24 October of 2021, the Animation Adaption of the game was officially announced and the first episode released on October 28 of 2022.
