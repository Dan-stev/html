About Arknights

Arknights take place in a post-apocalyptic dystopian science fantasy world known as Terra.

Natural disasters leave behind a valuable mineral, originium, which infects people with a progressive disease,

oripathy, but can also enhance the ability to use "Arts"(similar to magic).

Because oripathy is highly infectious and invariably fatal, the infected are shunned and persecuted.

In response, some of them form a group called the Reunion, a militant group waging war on the despotic governments of Terra.

Overview

In the world of Terra, a place ravaged by a mysterious mineral known as Originium.

While this mineral grants unique powers, it also brings a deadly infection, spreading chaos and fear among Terra's inhabitants.

The infected, often ostracized by society, find themselves in a desperate fight for survival

as governments and factions clash over the future of Terra.

The story centers around Rhodes Island,

a pharmaceutical and paramilitary organization devoted to researching Originium and aiding the infected.

Led by the young and empathetic Amiya, and guided by the strategic genius known only as the Doctor,

Rhodes Island aims to find a cure and challenge the stigma surrounding the infected.

Together, they face threats from Reunion, a militant group of infected people seeking revenge,

and navigate a world where survival and morality often clash.
